What is Nostr?

Nostr (Notes and Other Stuff Transmitted by Relays) is a decentralized, censorship-resistant social protocol.

Why do I need it for Nostrcoin? Nostrcoin uses Nostr events as its ledger. You need a Nostr account to mine or transfer NSTC.

To get started you'll need to:

  • Create a Nostr account
  • Install a signing extension (AKA Profiles, Alby, nos2x, Gooti, etc.)
